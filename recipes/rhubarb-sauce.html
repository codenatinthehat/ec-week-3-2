Rhubarb Sauce

A delicious rhubarb desert

This desert is an amazing treat after a meal. Personally one of my favorite foods.

Ingredients

  • 1/2-3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1lb rhubarb, cut into 1 in pieces, 4 cups
  • Ground Cinnamon, if desired

Instructions

Step 1

In 2-quart saucepan, heat sugar and water to boiling, stirring occasionally. Stir in rhubarb to reduce heat.

Simmer uncovered about 10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until rhubarb is tender and slightly transparent.

Step 2

Stir in cinnamon. Serve sauce warm or chilled.
